Hi. I'm Melisa.

I’m a former educator who’s dreaming of having a career in tech so I can keep learning new things about technology everyday, be creative and more in control of my job prospects. I wrote my "Hello World" on January 2019 and since then I have never stopped learning and forging my path to be a professional developer. I’m an enthusiastic and insatiable learner with adaptability skill and perseverance. I enjoy the bliss of solving problems and being able to create things with programming. Those things make me a logical thinker in mind, but also creative at heart. I'm also a kind of person who don't give up easily and I think of difficulties as a chance to build my competence. I started to learn programming by using free and/or affordable resources and courses to learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, front-end libraries (Bootstrap, jQuery, ReactJS), Python, Django, Git, and a bit SQL. I think my decision to be a developer is one of the best decisions I've made in life.
